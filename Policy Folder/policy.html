My name is Alexa, and I'm a certified nail technician with a passion for creating beautiful, lasting nail designs that enhance your style and confidence.

PAYMENTS

A $30 deposit is required to secure your appointment. This amount will go toward your final total and is non-refundable and non-transferable under any circumstance.

All major credit cards, debit cards, and cash are accepted.

CANCELLATION

Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours in advance.

Failure to do so will count as a no-show, and you will need to pay a $10 cancellation fee before booking your next appointment.

Life happens! Just give me a heads up so we can reschedule.

LATE ARRIVALS

Please arrive on time for your appointment. Late arrivals may result in a shortened service time or rescheduling.

If you're running more than 15 minutes late, your appointment may need to be rescheduled.

Respect for everyone's time keeps the schedule smooth!

HEALTH & SAFETY

All tools are sanitized and disinfected between clients following strict health regulations.

If you have any nail infections, allergies, or health concerns, please let me know before your appointment.

Your safety and hygiene are my top priorities!
